Egg Fried Rice

Home Page

Description

This recipe for Egg Fried Rice is perfectly suited for both beginners to cooking and experts who want to better their simple dishes. It'll take around 20 minutes total, so it'll be a perfectly quick dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/2 Tbsp salt
  • 1 cup of uncooked instant rice
  • Onion and egg

Steps

  1. Bring water, soy sauce, and salt to a boil in a medium saucepan. Stir in instant rice and remove from heat. Cover and let stand for 5 minutes.
