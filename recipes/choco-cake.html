Chocolate Cake

Home Page

Description

This recipe is beloved by the community for its amazing taste and pleasing look. It'll take around an hour, but will yield a large cake.

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Baking Soda & Baking Powder
  • Salt, eggs, and milk
  • Vegetable oil, vanilla extract, and Water

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round baking pans.
  2. Stir sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt together in a large bowl.
  3. Add eggs, milk, oil and vanilla; mix for 2 minutes on medium speed with an electric mixer.
  4. Stir in the boiling water. The batter will be thin. Pour evenly into the prepared pans.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 30 to 35 minutes. Cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
